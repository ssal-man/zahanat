About BUSy




City buses are one of  the best ways to get through a city but the thing with city buses which concerns a lot of daily commuters is that  city buses are unreliable the lack of tracking system and any real time data from the bus makes it a hard job to monitor  buses for the government and an unreliable source for people to get from one point to another.


Our main aim through this app is to simplify the tracking and making city buses more reliable for all the people. BUSy is a web app that can show real time data of a city bus in a map , alongwith estimated time of arrival at the boarding station for the commuter.
